Hi! I'm an AI researcher focused on building multimodal AI systems that are broadly useful and robust. I have a track record of leading high-impact projects from conception to deployment. At SDSU's MINT Lab, I designed and led the development of the Video Comprehension Score, Recurrent Multimodal Models, and the CLIP-CC Dataset, managing a team of three. Previously at Amazon, I led the development of the Frugal Event Detection device (FRED), and at Trilliant, I spearheaded the creation of point-to-point wireless IoT Water Meter Devices. I hold an M.S. in Computer Science (4.0 GPA) and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering, both from South Dakota State University.
