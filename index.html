Harsh Dubey, Chulwoo Pack

Preprint

TL;DR VCS evaluates long‑form descriptions via three components: Global Alignment (thematic), Local Alignment (semantic), and Narrative Alignment (chronological with tolerance). It detects corrupted content, maintains cross‑author consistency (>80%), and VCSshort achieves state‑of‑the‑art human correlation on VATEX‑EVAL (9‑ref) with competitive 1‑ref performance.

Leveraging Textual Memory and Key Frame Reasoning for Full Video Understanding Using Off‑the‑Shelf LLMs and VLMs (Student Abstract)

Harsh Dubey, Chulwoo Pack

AAAI 2025

TL;DR We integrate a VLM and LLM with a textual memory mechanism to maintain temporal continuity and context for fine‑grained long‑video understanding. We also introduce VAD‑Score to quantify precision, recall, and F1 for events, subjects, and objects. The approach achieves competitive results on DREAM‑1K across live‑action, animation, shorts, stock, and YouTube videos.
